Rosa, a microbiologist for the startup company Improbable Foods, is seeking to discover a species of bacteria that synthesizes proteins that taste like beef! After successfully synthesizing meat from other sources like algae, fungi, and plant roots, she's still on the hunt for the perfect taste. Rosa believes that there just might be the right bacteria for this project hiding out somewhere on a human body. She has decided to take navel samples from people across the country and assigned them anonymous identification numbers. This website is a projection of the experimental results run on the samples and may perhaps provide the participants with some interesting insights about their personal microbiome. Please, explore the data and see what you find!
